# Applicable Insurance Regulations

## State Insurance Code Requirements

### Section 2071 - Cancellation Notice
All commercial liability policies must provide a minimum of **30 days written notice** prior to cancellation for non-payment of premium, and **60 days written notice** for all other cancellations.

### Section 2074 - Policy Delivery
Insurers must deliver or mail the policy to the insured within **45 days** of the effective date.

### Section 2080 - Required Provisions
All commercial liability policies must include:
1. Clear statement of coverage limits
2. Definition of "insured"
3. Territory of coverage
4. Policy period
5. Duties in the event of occurrence or claim

### Section 2085 - Prohibited Provisions
The following provisions are prohibited:
1. Arbitration clauses that waive jury trial rights
2. Choice of law provisions selecting non-domicile state
3. Late notice disclaimers for prejudice-free delays

## Federal Requirements

### Terrorism Risk Insurance Act (TRIA)
- Disclosure of terrorism coverage
- Premium allocation for terrorism risk
- Policyholder election form

### ERISA Compliance (if applicable)
- Fiduciary requirements
- Disclosure standards
- Claims procedures

## Disclosure Requirements

### Premium Disclosure
- Itemized premium breakdown
- Terrorism surcharge (if any)
- State assessments and fees

### Coverage Disclosure
- Coverage summary in plain language
- Exclusions summary
- Claims reporting procedures

## Endorsement Standards

### Format Requirements
- Clear effective date
- Reference to policy modified
- Signature block (if required by state)

### Content Requirements
- Unambiguous language
- Consistent with base policy definitions
- Cannot create coverage gaps
